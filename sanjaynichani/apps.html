SQUEAK - Every PiCTURE HAS A STORY

Skqueak is a new mobile photo sharing paradigm. A Skqueak allows you to add voice, sketch, zoom and text to your pictures, to create a short animation clip that plays like a movie. Users can also reply using Skqueaks as opposed to just “Likes” and “Comments”, promoting rich conversations around pictures and taking user engagement to the next level. Every picture has a story, now that can be told with Skqueak. Squeak 16 is an App that enables rich conversations around pictures and short-form video. Please contact info@pelfunc.com for licensing of our Timeline SDK, technology and patent that powers Skqueak, Squeak16 and SqueakBack.
